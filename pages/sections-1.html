Rule Sections 1–5

SECTION 1 — The Constitution

  • This league operates under the supreme authority of the Commissioner (Noah).
  • Any challenge to the Commissioner's judgment must be accompanied by a $20 protest fee and a 500‑word MLA essay.

SECTION 2 — League Governance

  • League offices: Commissioner; Assistant to the Commissioner (optional, unpaid, decorative); Trash Talk Enforcement Unit (TTU); and League Historian (screenshots everything).

SECTION 3 — The Draft

  • Managers must be sober or convincingly pretending.
  • Auto‑drafting more than two rounds invites public ridicule captured for eternity by the Historian.

SECTION 4 — Entry Fees

  • Entry fees are due before Week 1.
  • Venmo notes like 'roof help' or 'feet pics' are discouraged and will age poorly.

SECTION 5 — Trades & Transactions

  • Trades must be football‑motivated between consenting managers.
  • Any trade made 'for the meme' may be investigated and roasted.
