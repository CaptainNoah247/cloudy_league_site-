Rule Sections 11–15

SECTION 11 — Tanking

  • Intentional tanking results in a mandatory Zach Wilson start for one week.

SECTION 12 — Hall of Shame Eligibility

  • Historic calamities may be enshrined, including 'The Great Achane Auction of 2025' and 'Pitts for $100'.

SECTION 13 — Investigations

  • The Commissioner may open investigations using screenshots, Venmo receipts, and witness memes as evidence.

SECTION 14 — League Votes

  • Majority rules unless the Commissioner exercises sanity override.

SECTION 15 — Commissioner Powers

  • Absolute and adaptable. Subject to change if Noah wakes up spicy.
