Rule Sections 6–10 Updated

SECTION 6 — Collusion

  • Collusion includes trading players for cash, goods, services, or emotional favors; coordinated tanking; and posting Venmo receipts in league chat.
  • Penalty: immediate loss of prize eligibility and public shaming.

SECTION 7 — The $100 Rule

  • Any trade involving real currency auto‑voids.
  • Sender = 'The Financier'. Receiver = 'The Morally Bankrupt'. Names stick all season.

SECTION 8 — Trade Review Process

  • The Commissioner may approve, veto, or roast. The standard is competitive integrity, not your feelings.

SECTION 9 — League Prizes

  • Payouts occur after the championship.
  • No prize for bribery, match‑fixing, or pyramid schemes.

SECTION 10 — Rivalries

  • Rivalries are encouraged.
  • Trash talk must remain clever—no 'my grandma scores more points' reruns.
