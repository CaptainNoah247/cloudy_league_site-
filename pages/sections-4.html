Rule Sections 16–20 Updated

SECTION 16 — Appeals Process

  • Appeals must be filed as a haiku or limerick.
  • Successful appeals are rare and celebrated with a single slow clap.

SECTION 17 — Trash Talk Regulations

  • Originality required. Keep it clever. GIF usage encouraged within taste and Terms of Service.

SECTION 18 — Psychic Drafting

  • Predicting injuries doesn't make you insightful; it makes you weird.

SECTION 19 — Injured Reserve Abuse

  • IR is for actually injured players. Using it otherwise is a fantasy misdemeanor.

SECTION 20 — Lineup Deadlines

  • Two missed lineups = probation. Three = mandatory *Draft Day* rewatch with commentary.
